# PD Webové-Mesto — Land. Tradition. Future.

A complex corporate and industrial multi-page presentation for a real-life model of an agricultural cooperative (founded in 1976). The website presents the transformation of traditional agriculture into a modern, technologically driven enterprise.

---

## About the PD Webové-Mesto project
This project represents a robust corporate presentation of the agricultural sector, which manages the cadastres of the municipalities *Web, Frontend Mestečko, Pixelovce and Renderovce*. The website combines a historical legacy (established by the merger of JRD Frontend and JRD Backend) with a modern presentation of crop production, livestock production (free-range breeding of 550 head of cattle), a mechanization center and an associated catering plant.

## Architecture and detailed structure of the website
The project stands out with its deep and detailed hierarchical structure, which goes beyond the framework of regular business cards:

### Main subpages:
- **Introduction (`index.html`):** Gateway with a strong corporate video/visual in the Hero section, key numerical statistics of the company and an overview of activities.
- **About us (`onas.html`):** History of the cooperative since 1976, the transformation process from the 90s and an introduction to the bodies of the cooperative.
- **Activity (`cinnost_druzstva.html`):** Central hub for the main production and service centers of the company.
- **News (`aktuality.html`):** Company blog/message board for announcements to partners and the public.
- **Contact (`kontakt.html`):** Contact details, billing information (IČO, DIČ), office hours and geographical location of the centers.

### Specific product branches (Centers):
- **Crop production (`pestovanie_plodin.html`):** Information on crop rotation (wheat, rapeseed, sugar beet) and irrigation management.
- **Animal production (`zivocisna_vyroba.html`):** Presentation of Holstein cattle breeding and raw milk production.
- **Mechanization (`mechanizacia.html`):** Overview of the company's technological park (fleet of John Deere combines, foragers and tractors) and service offer for external partners.
- **Company catering (`kuchyna.html`):** Information on the company's own kitchen, which supplies hot meals to employees and external customers.

## Design and Development Elements (UI/UX)
- **Industrial Typography and Cleanliness:** The website uses serious, highly readable typography suitable for state institutions, cooperatives and corporations.
- **Creative Storytelling:** Humorously incorporated IT terminology into the agricultural theme (*JRD Frontend*, *Renderovce*), which serves as the author's unique signature.
- **Responsive Design:** Intelligent navigation system (Hamburg menu for mobile phones) and flexible CSS grids that hold the structure firmly even on small displays.

## Technologies Used
The project was developed to achieve instant response and clean processing without the need for external JavaScript dependency libraries:

- **HTML5** – Advanced semantic linking of an extensive set of subpages.
- **CSS3** – Complex layout (CSS Grid, Flexbox), custom card designs and responsive optimization.
- **Vanilla JavaScript** – Navigation logic, mobile menu and dynamic state switching.
